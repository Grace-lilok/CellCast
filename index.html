Forecasting a Cell’s Future Before the Experiment

CellCast is an AI-assisted model that predicts how cells respond to perturbations directly from control-state single-cell gene expression profiles, reducing the need for costly and time-consuming laboratory perturbation experiments.

A full-gene axial-attention predictor

Model architecture

CellCast reshapes the full gene expression vector into a fixed two-dimensional gene grid, then predicts perturbation-induced residual expression changes.

  • Gene-aware tokens combine expression values, gene identity, and 2D position.
  • Row-wise and column-wise attention model dependencies across thousands of genes efficiently.
  • The predicted residual response is added back to the control profile to produce the perturbed expression state.
